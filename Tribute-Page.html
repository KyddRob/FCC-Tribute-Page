MF DOOM

BIOGRAPHY

Daniel Dumile, best known by his stage name MF DOOM, was a British-American hip hop artist and record producer. He was best known for his intricate wordplay and signature metal mask that resembled that of Marvel Comics villain Doctor Doom. By also blending elements from Destro and Phantom of the Opera, Dumile created a supervillain-like alter ego with its own backstory that he referenced in his music. The combination of his artistry, technical ability, and character development allowed him to become one of the most celebrated figures in underground and alternative hip hop. Sadly, Dumile passed away at the age of 49 on October 31, 2020. Continue for More on MF DOOM
